Player cards consists of two main elements, .btn-group with buttons to toggle the player cards, and the .game-player-card elements.

Toggle buttons need to have class name .btn-toggle-player-cards. Clicking on a toggle button toggles the class .flip-back on .game-player-card elements and determines which side of the cards is visible.

Player card elements must be inside container with class names .game-player-cards and .flip-game-player-cards. Player cards can contain information for 1-3 players and their contents are different depending on that amount. The front and back sides of the player cards are in wrappers .game-player-card-content.card-front and .game-player-card-content.card-back.

The back side of player cards is more generic with the only exception that when there are backbetters present, their bets are indicated with .game-player-bet-data-indicator elements.

Empty seats have class name .empty

Requires player-cards.js
