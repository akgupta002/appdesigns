Dropdown filters have two styles: Sliders and Checkboxes. There area two types of Sliders: Win/Loss and Utilization.

Slider filters have low / high values and min / max values. The initial values are set to spans .filters-slider-header-min and .filters-slider-header-min as well as in data-values array for the .filters-slider-range element

.filter-slider-footer contains labels and possibly scale for the slider.

Requires dropdowns.js and filters.js
